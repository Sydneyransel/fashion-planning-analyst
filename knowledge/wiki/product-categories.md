# SKIMS Product Categories

*Sources: skims.com collection pages (shapewear, bras, bodysuits, swim, loungewear, dresses, underwear), whowhatwear.com, harpersbazaar.com*

## Shapewear

SKIMS' founding category and still its highest-profile line. Shapewear is the keyword most associated with the brand in press coverage and Google search. Key product lines include sculpting bodysuits, high-waist shorts, and full-body solutions in the Sculpt and Smooth ranges.

**Positioning:** "Technically constructed shapewear that enhances your curves." Marketed as functional and body-positive — the goal is enhancement, not restriction.

**Price range:** ~$38–$128  
**Key fabrics:** Sculpt, Smooth, Fits Everybody  
**Press signal:** Who What Wear describes SKIMS shapewear as the category that "changed the game" — specifically calling out the seamless construction and the range of nude shades inclusive of darker skin tones.

**Demand pattern (Google Trends):** Consistently highest baseline interest of all SKIMS-related keywords. Peaks sharply in Q4 (November–December), consistent with holiday gifting behavior.

---

## Bodysuits

One of SKIMS' most commercially successful categories. Bodysuits bridge shapewear and everyday wear — they are worn both as base layers under clothing and as standalone tops. The Fits Everybody bodysuit line has become a signature product.

**Positioning:** Versatile enough for layering or standalone wear. Fits Everybody fabric is the anchor technology — stretches to twice its size.

**Price range:** ~$48–$88  
**Key fabrics:** Fits Everybody, Cotton Jersey, Cotton Rib  
**Press signal:** Frequently cited in Vogue as a wardrobe staple; credited with driving the "bodysuit renaissance" in mainstream fashion.

**Demand pattern:** Strong year-round interest, with elevated periods around major launches and collaborations (NikeSkims drove measurable search lift).

---

## Bras

Bras entered the SKIMS lineup as a natural extension of the shapewear and bodysuit core. The Fits Everybody bra line — built on the same stretch fabric — became a fast seller. Styles range from strapless to push-up to bralettes.

**Positioning:** Comfort-first bras engineered to fit a wide range of body types. Not heavily marketed through traditional Victoria's Secret-style imagery — more matter-of-fact and functional.

**Price range:** ~$34–$64  
**Key fabrics:** Fits Everybody, Naked, Smooth  
**Press signal:** Harper's Bazaar trial-reviewed the SKIMS bra and hip enhancer line, noting the brand's unusual transparency about body-shaping products.

**Demand pattern:** Moderate baseline with clear Q4 spike; closely correlated with shapewear trends.

---

## Underwear

SKIMS underwear covers thongs, hipsters, boy shorts, and briefs in Cotton Jersey, Lightweight Cotton, and Fits Everybody. Price points start at $12 (thong), making this the lowest entry price in the SKIMS lineup.

**Positioning:** Everyday essentials engineered to stretch, breathe, and last. Extended size range (XXS–5X).

**Price range:** ~$12–$28  
**Demand pattern:** Steady baseline interest year-round, less seasonal than shapewear or swim. Spikes with major sales events (Black Friday, SKIMS sale drops).

---

## Swim

SKIMS Signature Swim launched as a seasonal collection and has become a growing revenue category. Bikini tops, bikini bottoms, one-pieces, and cover-ups in branded silhouettes and colorways.

**Positioning:** "Signature Swim" — SKIMS-specific cut and fabric engineered for fit across body types, not just the traditional swimwear model archetype.

**Price range:** ~$44–$128  
**Demand pattern:** Strongly seasonal — search interest peaks June–July (summer season) and drops sharply in winter. Among the most predictable seasonal patterns in the keyword set.

---

## Loungewear

Loungewear is SKIMS' broadest category by SKU count — cotton sets, pajamas, sleep shirts, robes, and casual knits. The SKIMS Sleep sub-line and Cotton Rib range anchor this category.

**Positioning:** Premium basics for at-home wear. Positioned as luxurious but accessible — a direct competitor to Skims' own brand adjacents like Lunya and Eberjey.

**Price range:** ~$38–$168 (sets)  
**Key fabrics:** Cotton Jersey, Cotton Rib, SKIMS Sleep  
**Demand pattern:** Peaks in November–January (holiday gifting + winter). Lowest interest in June–August when swim dominates.

---

## Dresses

SKIMS dresses entered the lineup as the brand expanded into ready-to-wear. Styles include mini dresses, midi dresses, and bandage-style options in Fits Everybody and jersey fabrics.

**Positioning:** The brand's move into mainstream apparel beyond undergarments. Signals SKIMS' ambition to compete in the broader women's fashion market.

**Price range:** ~$68–$148  
**Demand pattern:** Among the lower-interest keywords in this dataset (compared to shapewear/bodysuit). Seasonal spikes around spring and event dressing season (April–June).
