## FAQS

### Do SKIMS bodysuits run true to size?

SKIMS bodysuits usually run true to size. Bodysuits that have jump sizes or unique sizing will have that information in the product details, so be sure to consult this info to make sure you take home your best fit.

### How do I know if my bodysuit fits?

Bodysuits are intended to skim the body, but they should not be overly tight. The seams shouldn’t cut into your skin, and it shouldn’t ride up in any way that feels uncomfortable. You should feel snatched and lifted, and your pants should feel like they fit better than ever, all without the fuss of tucking in or smoothing down a shirt.

### How to style a bodysuit

There are so many ways! That’s what we love about them. We suggest pairing your bodysuits with your favorite bottoms for an instantly sleek look—whether jeans, trousers, [sweatpants](https://skims.com/collections/leggings-pants), [skirts](https://skims.com/collections/skirts), [shorts](https://skims.com/collections/shorts), or [leggings](https://skims.com/collections/leggings-pants). Check out our “Complete The Look” suggestions to help you concept and style your best bodysuit looks.

For example, wear a bodysuit with a pencil skirt or jeans and a blazer or fuzzy cardigan for everyday sophistication–it’s a wrinkle-free, tucked-in look without any riding up. For the weekend, there’s nothing comfier than wearing a slimming bodysuit under [fleece-lined sweatpants](https://skims.com/products/cotton-fleece-classic-straight-leg-pant-light-heather-grey) and a matching [hoodie](https://skims.com/collections/hoodies-sweaters).

### Do you wear underwear with a bodysuit?

Most SKIMS bodysuits offer 100% cotton gussets, making underwear completely optional. So, to wear or not to wear underwear—when it comes to bodysuits—it’s all up to you.

### Can you wear a bra with a bodysuit?

Whatever makes you feel the best! SKIMS bodysuits can be worn with or without a bra, so it comes down to your personal support preference. If it’s a lightweight bodysuit, some customers like wearing a no-show bra underneath, like our [Fits Everybody T-Shirt Bra](https://skims.com/products/fits-everybody-t-shirt-bra-onyx). Other bodysuit styles, like those in our [Seamless Sculpt collection](https://skims.com/collections/seamless-sculpt), offer more built-in support and coverage.
