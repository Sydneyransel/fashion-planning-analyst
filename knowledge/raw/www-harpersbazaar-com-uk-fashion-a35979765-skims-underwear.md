There's a certain sense of snobbery at play when it comes to the celebrity fashion label (and often, for good reason). Many of us, particularly those who work in the fashion industry, are sceptical about celebrity-owned brands – no matter how successful or lucrative they are. There is an assumption that it is all hype and no substance, that it is merely an attempt to line their pockets further with no consideration of the customer.

So, despite the fact that I would actually consider myself a fan of Kim Kardashian West (and yes I have seen every episode of _Keeping Up_), I definitely still had reservations when it came to trying out her underwear label – despite the face that the reality star is responsible for creating several multi-million-dollar businesses.

And so, when I was offered the chance to try a set from Kardashian West’s shapewear and lingerie label Skims, I wasn’t expecting much – but I was pleasantly surprised. In fact, I couldn’t have been more wrong. I found Skims to be some of the most comfortable and supportive underwear I have ever worn.

The set I tried was from the [Fits Everybody range](https://go.redirectingat.com/?id=127X618962&url=https%3A%2F%2Fskims.com%2Fcollections%2Fbras%23fits%2520everybody). The briefs were super soft and hugged my hips perfectly – so far, so good – but it was the non-wired bralette that really impressed, giving me as much lift and support as I would expect from a wired bra. As a woman with a bigger bust, it's a struggle I'm all too familiar with; it's very difficult to find non-wired bras that offer enough support, particularly at this price point.

It turns out that the Fits Everybody range is Skims’ top-selling underwear – and its popularity is down to all the reasons that I loved it.

Kardashian West explains that with this range, she set out to create something that would feel like “bespoke underwear” to every woman that wears it.

“I could never find underwear that was comfortable or soft enough, or that didn’t dig,” she tells me. “I wanted to create a solution to that, which is how we came to develop Fits Everybody. This is truly the best underwear I have ever worn – it’s made from a buttery soft fabric that stretches to over twice its size while never losing shape – it just springs back and moulds to your body. We’ve innovated the material across all of our nine sizes, so it really feels like bespoke underwear made just for you. I love it and I honestly wear it every single day.”

Achieving the level of comfort and the support that is at the heart of these products comes from months and months of back and forth, Kardashian West explains.

“Comfort has always been my main priority and drives initial decisions on fabric and fit, as well as whether or not a product is ultimately ready to launch. If there needs to be further development or revisions, we will always take the time to do that so the fit and feel are perfect by the time they reach the customer.”

Of course, Fits Everybody is not Skims’ only product range, with each attempting to offer a different solution. The shapewear, for example, has been developed in an innovative way using a highly technical fabric that aims to enhance curves by offering targeted support in a nuanced way, rather than flattening a body like traditional shapewear did. The brand is constantly experimenting with fabric and fit to offer the best support.

“We take extreme care to ensure every piece delivers on its promise and effectively solves a problem,” she adds. “If it’s not perfect, we don’t launch it.”

Still feeling sceptical? Kardashian West has an answer for that.

“I would encourage anyone who has doubts about Skims to try it! We stand behind everything we do with confidence and I’m so grateful that I am able to turn my experience and passion for undergarments into solutions that women all around the world are relying on to look and feel amazing.”
