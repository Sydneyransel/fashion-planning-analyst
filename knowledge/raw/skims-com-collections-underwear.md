### FAQs

### How to wash underwear?

SKIMS underwear care varies by style and fabrication. Please reference the care instructions for each style online or on the care tag on your garments before washing your favorite pair. For example, some require cold water, others require hanging to dry.

### Do you wear underwear under shapewear?

[SKIMS shapewear](https://skims.com/collections/shapewear) can be worn with or without underwear, based on your preference. Most of our shapewear includes a 100% cotton gusset, making underwear optional for these styles. But if you want to protect your shapewear or you just feel better wearing underwear, adding a [lightweight cheeky panty](https://skims.com/collections/cheeky-underwear) or a [thong](https://skims.com/collections/thongs) is a great choice.

### Does SKIMS underwear run small?

SKIMS underwear runs true to size. Reference our [size guide](https://skims.com/pages/size-guides) for the best fit.

### How can I understand my underwear measurements?

**For high-waisted underwear styles**, we suggest measuring your waist size at the smallest point of the waist—above the hips. Then, reference our [size guide](https://skims.com/pages/size-guides) to match the size that best corresponds with your measurements.

**For mid-rise and low-rise underwear styles**, we suggest taking your hip measurement at the widest point of your hips—above your thighs. Then, reference our [size guide](https://skims.com/pages/size-guides) to match the size that best corresponds with your measurements.
